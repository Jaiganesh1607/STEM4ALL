Introduction to Robotics

This workshop provides a comprehensive introduction to the field of robotics, covering fundamental concepts, design principles, and practical applications. Participants will learn about the history of robotics, different types of robots, and their components, including sensors, actuators, and control systems. Through hands-on activities and projects, students will gain experience in building and programming simple robots, fostering creativity and problem-solving skills. The workshop also explores the ethical considerations and societal impacts of robotics, preparing students for future advancements in the field.
