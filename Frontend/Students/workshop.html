Introduction to Robotics

Explore the fundamentals of robotics, including mechanics, electronics, and programming. Build and program your own mini-robot to navigate a simple maze.

Workshop Details

This workshop is designed for students aged 14-18 with an interest in engineering and technology. No prior experience is required. Participants will learn about the key components of a robot, how to assemble them, and how to write code to control their movements. The workshop culminates in a friendly competition where students test their robots' navigation skills.

Instructor

Dr. Anya Sharma

Robotics Engineer & Educator

Dr. Sharma has over 10 years of experience in robotics and has taught at several universities. She holds a PhD in Robotics from MIT and is passionate about making STEM education accessible to all students.
